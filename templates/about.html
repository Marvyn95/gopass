About Us

Our Mission

GoPass is dedicated to revolutionizing the way you experience events. Our mission is simple yet powerful: to make ticket booking seamless, secure, and accessible for everyone. With a passion for technology and a commitment to user satisfaction, we provide a platform where attendees can easily discover, book, and manage tickets for a wide range of events.

Who We Are

Our team brings together expertise in event management and digital innovation to ensure every ticket is authentic and every entry is smooth. Whether you’re organizing a concert, conference, or community gathering, GoPass empowers you with tools for effortless event management and unforgettable experiences.

At GoPass, we believe in the power of events to bring people together. Every ticket is more than just access—it’s a gateway to connection, culture, and community.

What We Offer

  • Discover Events Explore concerts, conferences, festivals, and community gatherings—all in one place.
  • Seamless Ticket Booking Book single or multiple tickets effortlessly from the comfort of your home. Each digital ticket comes with a unique signature for authenticity and instant validation at entry.
  • Empower Event Organizers Publish events, sell and validate tickets, and streamline attendee access with ease. Our platform makes event ticket management simple, efficient, and secure for organizers and attendees alike.
  • Secure & Reliable Platform GoPass uses advanced security protocols to protect your data and transactions. Enjoy peace of mind with encrypted payments, fraud prevention, and real-time ticket validation for every event.

Key Features

  • Event Creation Publish event details in minutes.
  • Online Ticket Sales Secure, fast, and mobile-friendly.
  • Ticket Validation QR code scanning ensures smooth entry.
  • Analytics Dashboard Track sales, attendance, and revenue in real time.
  • Multi-Device Access Cross platform web application designed for both desktop and mobile.

Why GoPass?

GoPass is more than a ticketing solution—it’s a robust platform for digital ticketing, event promotion, and real-time validation. We’re committed to transforming the event experience for both organizers and attendees, making ticketing, event management, and entry seamless, secure, and enjoyable for everyone.

Join us on our journey to transform event experiences, one ticket at a time.

Payment Details

GoPass offers secure and convenient payment options for all users. You can pay for your tickets using MTN MoMo Pay and Airtel Money. These trusted mobile money services ensure fast, reliable, and safe transactions, making it easy for you to complete your purchase from anywhere.

  • MTN MoMo Pay: Pay directly from your MTN mobile money account for instant ticket confirmation.
  • Airtel Money: Use your Airtel Money wallet for a seamless and secure payment experience.

Your payment information is always protected with industry-standard security protocols, so you can book your tickets with confidence.
